About Water Quality

Water is one of the Earth's most vital resources, essential for all forms of life and critical to numerous aspects of human society. The quality of water, determined by its chemical, physical, biological, and radiological characteristics, plays a pivotal role in shaping the health of ecosystems and the well-being of communities. Water quality is a multifaceted concept that reflects the cleanliness, safety, and suitability of water for various purposes, from drinking and irrigation to industrial processes and recreation.

Access to safe drinking-water is essential to health, a basic human right and a component of effective policy for health protection. This is important as a health and development issue at a national, regional and local level.
